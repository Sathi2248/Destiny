Ettina Bhuja

Best Tme to Visit : September - May

Embark on a thrilling trek to Ettina Bhuja, nestled in Chikkamagaluru's breathtaking landscapes. Starting at Nanya Bhairaveshwara Gudi, the tranquil trail winds through lush forests, offering panoramic vistas. Reaching a height of 1300 meters, the summit unveils an awe-inspiring view of rolling hills and the Western Ghats. As you venture forth.
remember to prioritize safety and preserve the natural beauty by refraining from plastic pollution.

Learn More:

  1. Prepare adequately: Carry essential trekking gear, sufficient water, snacks, and first aid supplies. Check weather conditions before embarking on the trek.
  2. Stay on marked trails: Stick to designated paths to avoid getting lost and damaging the fragile ecosystem.
  3. Be cautious and aware: Watch your step, especially in steep or slippery sections. Keep an eye out for loose rocks and use proper footwear for better grip.
  4. Respect wildlife and vegetation: Observe animals from a safe distance and avoid disturbing their natural habitats. Do not pluck flowers or damage plants.
  5. Leave no trace: Carry a trash bag and pack out all your waste. Dispose of it responsibly in designated bins after the trek. Refrain from littering or leaving behind any non-biodegradable items.
  6. Say no to plastic pollution: Minimize the use of single-use plastics. Bring reusable water bottles and containers for snacks. Dispose of any waste properly and encourage others to do the same.
By Road
  • Start from Ettina Bhuja, Chikkamagaluru.
  • Take the road towards Chikkamagaluru city.
  • From Chikkamagaluru, head south on State Highway 57.
  • Continue on SH 57 until you reach the city of Hassan.
  • From Hassan, take National Highway 75 (NH 75) towards Bangalore.
  • Follow NH 75 all the way to Bangalore.
By Public Transportation
  • From Ettina Bhuja, Chikkamagaluru, you can hire a local taxi or auto-rickshaw to Chikkamagaluru bus station.
  • At the bus station, you can find regular buses operated by Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC) or private bus operators going to Bangalore.
  • Board the appropriate bus to Bangalore and enjoy the scenic journey.
  • Buses typically take around 5-6 hours to reach Bangalore, depending on traffic and road conditions.
  • Once in Bangalore, you can arrange for local transportation or take a taxi to your desired destination within the city.

The time taken to complete the trek to Ettina Bhuja can vary depending on several factors, including the fitness level of the trekkers, the pace of the group, and the trail conditions. On average, it takes approximately 4 to 6 hours to reach the summit and return to the starting point at Nanya Bhairaveshwara Gudi. This estimation includes time for rest breaks, taking in the scenery, and enjoying the overall experience of the trek. It's important to plan accordingly and allow ample time to complete the trek at a comfortable pace.
